Our Mission

K.E. Sullivan (K.E.S) Foundation aims to change lives and transform our community by providing veteran outreach programs to assist homeless and disabled veterans, seniors, and youth with affordable housing, food, clothing, and establishment of benefits through training and mentorship.

Build a better tomorrow

Every dollar donated brings us closer to our goal. Your financial support allows us to fund essential renovations, making a tangible difference in the lives of those we serve.

Current Projects

We are in the process of restoring vacant units on the 2200 block of Druid Hill Avenue in Baltimore, Maryland. Our objective is to convert these buildings into multifunctional facilities aimed at reducing homelessness among veterans and aiding seniors in securing affordable housing. With a steadfast commitment to our community, we have set an ambitious goal to acquire 20-30 row homes within the next year.

Future Projects

Our vision is the formation of a utopia. Not your ordinary retirement community, but one that bridges the divide and unites people of all ages, providing disabled veterans with a sense of belonging in the world they once defended, a sanctuary for seniors to rediscover their youthful spirit, and a place for young people.
